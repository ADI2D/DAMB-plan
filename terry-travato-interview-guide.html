Fractional Ads Manager Interview Guide

Terry Travato — February 5, 2026
Attendees: Alex Bach, Daniel Stein, Dwayne Stoltzfus

Core Requirements to Confirm

Cost & Hours

  • ☐ Hourly rate? (Posted range: $100-150/hr)
  • ☐ Expected hours per week? (Target: 5-10 hrs/week)
  • ☐ Monthly retainer or hourly billing?
  • ☐ Any additional costs (tools, software, creative)?

Multi-Location Experience

  • ☐ Specific franchise or multi-location clients managed?
  • ☐ How do you structure campaigns for corporate vs. local?
  • ☐ Per-franchisee reporting — can you deliver location-level data?

Meta Ads Manager Strategy & Structure

  • ☐ Walk us through your typical campaign structure for lead gen
  • ☐ How do you handle TOF prospecting vs. BOF retargeting?
  • ☐ Creative testing methodology?

Technical Capability (Critical)

CAPI Implementation

  • ☐ Have you personally implemented Meta Conversions API?
  • ☐ Walk us through your process for setting it up
  • ☐ How do you validate data is flowing correctly?
Why it matters: Our #1 priority is fixing attribution. Previous manual uploads dropped CPL 30%+. We need someone who can actually implement CAPI, not just talk about it.

Attribution & Measurement

  • ☐ What attribution methodology do you use? (first-touch, last-touch, multi-touch)
  • ☐ How do you reconcile Meta's reported numbers with CRM reality?
  • ☐ How do you handle iOS 14.5+ privacy restrictions?
  • ☐ View-through conversions — how do you account for these?

Performance Proof

Results & Metrics

  • ☐ What CAC/CPL improvements have you achieved? (Specific numbers, not just percentages)
  • ☐ Largest monthly ad budget managed end-to-end?
  • ☐ What were the results? (CAC, CPL, ROAS, conversion rates)
Our current state: $150 CAC on cold ads, ~$72 CPL on franchise leads (trending up). Target: $50-60 CPL.

Declining Campaign Turnaround

  • ☐ Our current campaigns are trending down. Tell me about a time you diagnosed and fixed declining performance.
  • ☐ What's your process when a campaign suddenly spikes CPL 40%?

Speed vs. Attention to Detail

Speed Test

  • ☐ Walk me through your first 30 minutes diagnosing a campaign that suddenly tanked
  • ☐ How quickly can you turn around a new creative test once we identify a winning angle?
  • ☐ What's your typical turnaround time for launching a new campaign?

Detail Test

  • ☐ What's your QA process before launching a new campaign or major change?
  • ☐ Tell me about a time you caught a mistake that would have cost real money
  • ☐ How do you ensure tracking is set up correctly before spending?

The Balance

  • ☐ How do you decide when something needs more testing vs. when to just ship it?
  • ☐ What's an acceptable error rate when you're moving fast?
What we want to hear: "I move fast but I have checklists" — not "I'm a perfectionist" (too slow) or "I trust my gut" (too sloppy)

Dual Mandate: Franchise Sales + Local Athlete Leads

Scope Clarification: Michael handles Certification ads (online courses). This role owns Athlete leads (local franchisee) + Franchise leads (corporate). Three separate funnels, two for this hire.
  • ☐ How would you structure campaigns when we have two different buyer types?
    • Franchise buyers: Prospective franchisees (corporate leads)
    • Athlete families: Parents of individuals with special needs (local leads)
  • ☐ How do you prevent cannibalization between the two?
  • ☐ Can you deliver both org-wide insights AND location-level performance data?
  • ☐ Experience managing multiple distinct funnels simultaneously?

Working Style & Collaboration

Stakeholder Management

  • ☐ You'll have multiple stakeholders (CEO, investor, VP Marketing). How do you handle different opinions?
  • ☐ What does your reporting look like? (Format, frequency, level of detail)
  • ☐ Dashboard access — do we get real-time visibility?

Philosophy on Scaling

  • ☐ When is the right time to increase ad budget?
  • ☐ Our current recommendation is to optimize existing spend before increasing. Your take?
Red flag: Pushing for budget increases before proving optimization wins

Communication Style

  • ☐ How do you handle urgent issues? (Response time expectations)
  • ☐ Preferred communication channel?
  • ☐ How do you proactively surface problems vs. waiting to be asked?

Availability & Logistics

  • ☐ Current client load — can you consistently deliver 5-10 hrs/week?
  • ☐ Any capacity constraints in the next 90 days?
  • ☐ When can you start?
  • ☐ Have you worked with appointment-driven businesses? (Fitness, healthcare, therapy)

Red Flags to Watch For

⚠️ Vague on CAPI — Can't explain implementation specifics
⚠️ Lead volume focus — Talks quantity without mentioning lead quality or booked appointments
⚠️ Can't articulate franchise-specific experience — Generic multi-account isn't the same
⚠️ "My way or highway" attitude — Collaboration concerns given multiple stakeholders
⚠️ Pushes budget increase first — Should want to optimize current spend first
⚠️ No concrete numbers — Only speaks in percentages or vague "significant improvements"
